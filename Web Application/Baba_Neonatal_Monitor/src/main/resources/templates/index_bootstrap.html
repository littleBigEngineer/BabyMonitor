BABA - Neonatal Monitoring System

Baba is a neonatal monitoring system designed to increase the security and safety of infants, while keeping the parents confidence in mind. Baba is a sensory device that will monitor your infants enviornmental conditions. Parameters such as Temperature, Humidity, Gas levels. Baba also offers features such as live video and audio streaming, abilility to playlullabies for infants.

Baba has been designed with state of the art sensors, developed with efficiency in mind. The overall aim of the system is to provide a higher level of care for infants, while not increasing the workload of already busy parents. Baba comes with a companion application which is available on the Google Play Store. With this application the parent can monitor their infant from the comfort of their couch, or even on the go.

Your infants safety is the #1 priority!
